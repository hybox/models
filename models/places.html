Places

Introduction

The model follows DPLA MAPv4, assuming a base compatibility with geonames:Feature (this makes it a subclass of schema:Place).

Per #33, geometry information has been declared out of scope for the first phase.

Model

  • edm:Place
    • rdfs:label (MUST)
    • wgs84_pos:lat (SHOULD)
    • wgs84_pos:long (SHOULD)
    • wgs84_pos:alt (MAY)
    • gn:parentFeature (MAY)
    • gn:countryCode (MAY)
